# Privacy Policy
**G-Bay Boating Apps**  
**App:** Georgian Bay Boating Weather  
**Effective Date:** <EFFECTIVE_DATE>

G-Bay Boating Apps ("we", "us", "our") provides the Georgian Bay Boating Weather app (the "App").  
This Privacy Policy explains what information the App uses and how it is handled.

## 1. Information We Use

### a) Location Information
If you grant location permission, the App uses your device location to:
- determine the nearest marine area,
- show marine weather and warning context relevant to your area.

You can deny or revoke location permission at any time in device settings.

### b) App Settings and Local Cache
The App stores certain information **on your device** to function properly and improve usability, including:
- unit preferences (wind/wave units),
- safety threshold settings,
- locally cached forecast/observation data,
- timestamp metadata related to refresh behavior.

## 2. Information We Do Not Require for Use
The App does not require account registration or sign-in.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal information.

The App retrieves weather/warning data from third-party public sources (for example, forecast/observation and official marine warning pages) in order to display weather and marine context.

## 4. Permissions
The App may request:
- Internet access (to fetch weather/warning data),
- Location access (to provide nearby marine area relevance).

## 5. Data Retention
Data described above is primarily stored on-device and may be cleared by:
- uninstalling the App,
- clearing App storage in Android settings.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
The App is not specifically directed to children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the Effective Date above.
